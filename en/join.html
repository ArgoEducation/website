Join Us

Your primary function will be to mentor and guide talented Chinese high school students who strive to attend American universities. This function entails (but is certainly not limited to) college application mentoring and SAT/ACT/TOEFL/AP/English instruction.

You will also contribute directly to the startup's success through business/marketing/curriculum development. You will have the flexibility and freedom to hone your strengths and develop your weaknesses in our startup culture.

Your annual pay will be equivalent to 65K-75K USD before tax. Compensation package includes travel allowance, paid vacation time, housing allowance, health insurance and an annual appraisal bonus. There are always options to later take on expanded/other roles within Argo Education, especially as a startup, our partnership is open to committed and capable entrepreneurs at the moment.

Come join our mission at Argo Education!

Requirements:

  • English level: Native speaker; SAT 760+ on CR session
  • Graduate of a leading US university
  • Commitment and enthusiasm in Education
  • Comfortable to work in small teams and flexible work environment
  • Great sense of punctuality, responsibility and entrepreneurship
