About Us

An educational services and consulting company, Argo Education is committed to being the best in helping talented Chinese high school students reach their dreams of attending American universities and high schools. Through education and mentorship, Argo bridges the gap between Chinese education and American education and guides prospective students through the sometimes confusing and challenging application process. From the college application itself to SAT / TOEFL / AP / standardized-test preparation, Argo first and foremost views the students' success as the company's success.

Argo Education is founded in the heart of Shanghai, a renowned international city that is a bastion for limitless excitement, unforgettable experiences, and continual discoveries. Argo's own company culture reflects those things, as the strong entrepreneurial culture is led by integrity, motivation, and commitment.
