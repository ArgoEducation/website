Mission Statement

We are an education consulting firm based in Shanghai, China. Including but not limited to standardized-test preparation, college application essays, extracurricular activities guidance, mocking interview, we offer individualized one-stop service packages for high-end clients applying for high schools and/or colleges in the United States. All of our counselors graduated from the top universities in the US. The core values of ours center upon the principles of integrity, trustworthiness, professionalism, fairness, and respect. It is the mission of Argo to provide the highest quality professional and objective analysis and recommendations in the areas of academic enhancement, college preparation, and application strategy designed to help students improve their college admission competitiveness and achieve short/long-term educational life goals.
