The A-Team

Gerald Bradshaw: Executive Director

As a Harvard Law alumni, Bradshaw had been Harvad’s admission interviewer for 15 years and later have been working as an independent counsellor for over 20 years. With the knowledge of college admission system, expertise of students assessment and understanding of admission officers’ mind sets, Bradshaw has successfully sent students to Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Stanford and many other leading colleges and universities in the US. He not only guided his students through their application process, but also believes that it is important to lead them to a successful life as a mentor, thus many of his students have already become leaders in their area of industries.

Ying Chen: Career Planning Counsellor

Graduated from a prestige British boarding school Glenalmond College, Ying Chen fellowed his dream to the "Big Apple" to study Math, Economics and Philosophy at Gallatin School of Individualized Study, New York University. He fundraised, educated and organized various activities for Chinese orphans as the co-president of China Care at NYU. With his experience in charity, investment banking and education around the globe, he is dedicated to stimulate students' full potential and help them find the career plan that fits the most.

Jun Chen: Director of English Learning Program

Jun was born in Beijing and grow up in the State of Indiana. He had one of the most successful application results when he was a high school senior, being offered by Yale and Stanford, and chose to go to Dartmouth to study Mathematics and English Literature. For years in the line of English teaching, he inspire students using words to best present themselves in front of the admission officers. Every student of his sees him as their best friends for his dedication and passion in education.

Ray Yang: Education Counsellor

From ECNU second affiliated high school to Peddie School and Rice University, Ray has immersed himself with the best education in China and the US. Thus, he has his very own understanding of educational systems on both sides of the ocean and developed his very own way of application assistance methodology which helped hundreds of students enrolling schools of their dreams. He is determined to provide the most professional and innovative strategy and service for Chinese students to pursue their educational goals.
